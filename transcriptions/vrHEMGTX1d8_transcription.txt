Steps to clean grapes

- Place your grapes in a large bowl and add 2 teaspoons of baking soda and 2 teaspoons of salt.
- Cover with cold water, mix it around, and let it soak for 5 minutes.
- This is the leftover dirty water. Rinse them off now and savor your shiny, clean grapes.
- It eliminate all the grime, but it also gets rid of that dull waxy film.
